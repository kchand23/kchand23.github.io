Krishna Vamsi Chandu


Welcome to my website. I am Krishna Vamsi, a computer science undergraduate seeking internship opportunities as a software developer. I have been doing research work for Wildbook Project at the Computational Biology lab at University of Illinois at Chicago for over two years. The primary aim of my research is to find out whether an accurate assumption of animal population can be made using images found on social media. I have done plenty of projects for this particular research which I have listed them below with their corresponding links. With successful application of data structures, computer algorithms, object oriented programming and various frameworks, I consider myself as a highly motivated student with an immense love for programming. I am also experienced in software development technologies, Data base management systems, web development technology, Machine learning and deep learning techniques. As a computer scientist and engineer, my primary aim was, and will always be to impact people's life and solve real world problems with my experience and skills in programming.
